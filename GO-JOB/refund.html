Refund Policy - Apni Pathsala

Thank you for choosing Apni Pathsala. Our refund policy is designed to ensure your satisfaction and provide clarity on the terms of refunds.

1. Refund Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund, the following conditions must be met:

  • The request is made within 30 days of the course purchase.
  • No more than 20% of the course content has been viewed.

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our support team at support@apnipathsala.com with your request.
  2. Provide your order details and reason for the refund.
  3. We will review your request and respond within 5 business days.

3. Refund Processing

If your refund is approved, it will be processed within 10 business days. The refund amount will be credited to the original payment method.

Note: Refunds are not applicable for courses purchased during promotional events or on discounted rates.
