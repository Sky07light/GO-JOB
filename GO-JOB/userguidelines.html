User Guidelines - Apni Pathsala

Thank you for choosing Apni Pathsala! To ensure a positive and productive learning experience, please adhere to the following user guidelines:

1. Respectful Communication

Interact with fellow learners, instructors, and staff with courtesy and respect. Foster a positive and inclusive online community.

2. Academic Integrity

Ensure all submitted work is your own. Plagiarism and cheating are strictly prohibited and may result in disciplinary actions.

3. Participation

Actively engage in discussions, forums, and assignments. Your contributions enhance the learning experience for everyone.
